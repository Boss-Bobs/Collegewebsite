Non-Discrimination Statement

Effective Date: October 11, 1990

Springfield College is committed to providing an educational environment free from discrimination. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, disability, or age in our programs and activities.

We encourage diversity and inclusion within our community and provide equal opportunities for all students and staff.
