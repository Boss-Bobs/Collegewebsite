Admissions

Welcome to the admissions section! We are excited that you are considering joining our community. Here, you will find all the information you need about the application process, requirements, and resources to help you succeed.

Application Process

Our application process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Choose Your Program: Review our academic offerings and select the program that best fits your interests.
  2. Complete the Application: Fill out the application form and submit the required documents.
  3. Submit Transcripts: Provide official transcripts from all previously attended institutions.
  4. Letters of Recommendation: Submit at least two letters from individuals who can speak to your qualifications.
  5. Interview (if required): Some programs may require an interview. You will be notified if this is necessary.

Important Dates

Keep track of these important application deadlines:

  • Fall Semester: Application Deadline: April 15
  • Spring Semester: Application Deadline: October 15
  • Late Applications: Accepted until May 1 for Fall and November 1 for Spring.
